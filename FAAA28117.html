The Pegasus Robot will come pick up the Toothbrushes in the small local Warehouse nearby, and then the system will create him a logistic path to follow to optimise his delivery shift. This is the final step of our Industrial System, the delivery to the customer. This is the ground case delivery. We will use this to deliver customer in a dense city environnement (for exemple Paris). We use the Pegasus Cargo technology combined with the Amazon key system.
