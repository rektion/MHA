It is an ecologic, affordable and reliable system to deliver to individuals.
This droid has the main capability of transporting heavy loads (40kg to 500kg). It is useful for individuals, to carry groceries for example but also for professionals on a construction site or for delivery. The droid has two modes : one is follow me , the droid must follow someone while carrying his things (the operator can also control the droid remotely by 4G) and the second is more for delivery, the droid has the mission to go somewhere on its own. His autonomy is of only a day.
