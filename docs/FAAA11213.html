AgenceCobot is a recruitment agency for factory robots. The main goal of this service is to help small companies automating their factories by providing them the perfect suitable solution. The agency sends someone to the client s factory and the consultant will analyze and propose the best solution to automate the hard work of the plant depending on the plant s domain. They obviously first promote their MIP robots, but they say no option is excluded to find the perfect solution (even rival s robots).
