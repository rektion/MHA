An ultra-robot assembly line is born in Germany. It ends in autoguided vehicles to carry assemblies out of the factory. Here it is the airplane fuselage which is assembled by twenty robots. Each of them receive all components they work on by a ceiling rail which cover all the factory. Fuselage then leave to another factory.
