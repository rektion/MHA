Toothbrushes finish on a conveyor belt and fall into a box. This box is on a pallet that contains several boxes and turn when rotate when one is full to fill all boxes. When all boxes are full, another row of boxes is put on the one that have been filled and closed. Pallets are on a track like at the beginning of the factory. Once complete, pallets move on the traks to the departure platform. When a truck arrives to take on the packages the pallets got on the truck to be deliver to a distribution center.
