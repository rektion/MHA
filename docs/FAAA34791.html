Cement is a hydraulic binder (which hardens under the action of water), used in the preparation of concrete, and today most often used in the manufacture of pavements, concrete blocks, plasters and mortars.
Certain cements have specific properties, which allow them to adapt to particular environments (taking at sea, waters with a high sulphate content, etc.).
Other cements meet special conditions of use (very fast hardening, resistance to high heat or chemical attack), such as molten aluminous cement, very resistant to acidic media.
