FICHE DU SUJET STAN (Parking solution)


[membre de]
TECHNOLOGY REFINED SHEET

[voir le texte]
Stan is an outdoor robotic parking solution for cars. The autonomous robot lifts and carrys the car to a parking spot available. For now the solution is designed for airports, you just drop your car and Stan will do the rest for you.

design by

Stanley Robotics

release on (date)

End of 2015

use for (in our project)

We could use this to park cars, after the production line. It could either park the cars in a storage facility or directly put the cars in the trains/trucks for the customer shipment.

way of improving

Adapt the system to load the cars in boat/truck/train
Autonomy
Extend the system to indoors environment

has ecological impact
