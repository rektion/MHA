FICHE DU SUJET Tesla Semi is a prototype of a 100% electric semi-trailer truck that was presented on November 16, 2017 and whose production is scheduled for 2020 by Tesla. The manufacturer has announced that the truck will have an autonomy of 805 km with a full charge of its batteries, and that it could travel 640 km after a charge of 30 minutes which would give it 80% of its total charge when connected to a Tesla Megacharger solar charging station. Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla, said the base version of the Semi would come with Tesla autopilot, which enables semi-autonomous driving on highways.
