FICHE DU SUJET The aggregate is a fragment of rock, less than 125 mm in size (sand, gravel and gravel), intended to be used in the composition of materials intended for the manufacture of works of public works, civil engineering and building. It is the first underground resource exploited in France with 379 million tonnes extracted in 20111. It is the mineral resource most used by an average American citizen (with 562 tonnes per person)
