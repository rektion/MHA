The idea would be to develop an AI capable of identifying the most important things in the forest : the Bamboo blossom phenomenom and the growth of Bamboo shoots. In order to create such an AI we would need to train it manually from pictures of Bamboo forest soils (with a database of a minimum of 10 000 pictures).
With our AI, the autonomous robot can replace the gardener. The robot would make daily shift in and around our forests to locate the Bamboo shoots (so we can collect them) and detect a possible Bamboo blossom phenomenom.
