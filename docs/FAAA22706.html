FICHE DU SUJET At this step dry bamboo are put on a gutter one by one. They go through two cutting operation. The first one cut the bamboo in staps with something shape like an apple cutter. The width of the cut must be a bit more than the width of a toothbrush because it will be machined after. The second cut is vertical, the blade cut a toothbrush length, like the first cut it must be a little bit longer than a toothbrush to allow it to be machined.
