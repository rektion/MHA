FICHE DU SUJET Designed by a Russian engineer named Nikita Chen-yun-tai, the Apis Cor is a 3D printer measuring 4 1.6 1.5 m for more than 2 tonnes. Like many competitors printing from the inside, the machine is equipped with a robotic and telescopic arm allowing it to superimpose layers of concrete on a maximum surface of 132 square meters and up to 3 meters in height.
