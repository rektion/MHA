FICHE DU SUJET In our company of bamboo toothbrush, the quality check will of course be led by autonomous robots. First the factory would determine all criteria of quality that must be check. If the process is quick enough it could be performed on a big sample. For example, they would check the toothbrush shape with a laser, stiffness of the toothbrush and a few other criteria. If the toothbrush is considered good to be send it goes directly to packaging. But if it nonconform it goes to rework workshop.
