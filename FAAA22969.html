Nylon bristles have already been put by pack and slide into a very little metal ring. Then we take the toothbrush on a gripper again and drill little holes into the head of the size of the metal rings. Bristles are then put into those holes and must hold firmly. Final step of the fabrication is to trim bristle so they are all the same size and don't hurt customer gum.
