This delivery center is partially auntomated, more than 4000 robots in 152 000 m 2 of warehouse. However there are still humans, the warehouse has more than 3000 employees, mainly pickers. Those robots allow pickers not to walks severals kilometers per day just to fill packages. then they also allow to deliver more customers and to deliver a lot quicker than before (in 24h and even 12h when possible). Robots are 4 to 5 times quicker than humans, plus they don't get tired or sick so they don't need breaks.
