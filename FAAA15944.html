FICHE DU SUJET Amazon key is a system composed of a Key smart lock and a Cloud cam. The main goal of the system is to be able to deliver your package even when you are not home. The delivery guy scans the barcode of the package with the camera. If the location and the barcode matchs with your order, the door unlocks itself and the delivery guy can put the package safe in your home while being filmed by the camera. With this system you can also unlock your door anywhere at anytime with the app.
