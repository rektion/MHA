Naïo technologies developed 3 Autonomous Agricultural Robots, Oz, Dino and Ted. Each of this robots are designed to be mechanical weed killer by hoeing the soil. Oz is made for small land, Dino for real farmers and Ted is made for vine culture. It is a real time saving solution for farmers, and a good solution for soil pollution because farmers will be less dependant on chemical weed killers.
