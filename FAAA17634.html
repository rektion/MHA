FICHE DU SUJET Robots in data centers are emerging. They are use to manage cable, fiber, heat temperature etc... in data centers. Using sensors to move (on rails most of the time) in the center, they can displace components and detect problems. In some cases (ROME by Wave2Wave's) a robot can do in one minute the job that would have been done in 10 days by manual process.
